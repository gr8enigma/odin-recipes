Mojito

Description

Imagine sitting at a bar on a tropical island, watching the sun set on a beautiful day.
The air is still hot and your thirst after something refreshing. Enter: the Mojito.

The Mojito is a traditional Cuban cocktail typically consisting of just five ingredients.
It's combination of sweet, citrus and mint flavors has made it a summer drink favorite all over the world.

Ingredients

  • 5 cl White Rum
  • 2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 Lime
  • 6 cl Soda water
  • 8 Mint leaves
  • Crushed Ice (as needed)

Steps

  1. Cut the ends of the lime and cut it in 8 wedges. Place the mint leaves and half the lime wedges in a longdrink glas.
    Use a muddler to crush the mint and limes wedges.
  2. Add the remaining lime and sugar to the glass. Use the muddler again to release the remaining lime juices.
  3. Fill the glass three quarters with ice.
  4. Pour in the rum and fill up the glass with soda.
  5. Garnish with mint leaves and add sugar to taste.
  6. Enjoy!
