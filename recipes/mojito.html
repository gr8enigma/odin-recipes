Mojito

Description

Imagine sitting at a bar on a tropical island, watching the sun set on a beautiful day. The air is still hot and your thirst after something refreshing. Enter: the Mojito.

The Mojito is a traditional Cuban cocktail consisting of just five ingredients. It's combination of sweet, citrus and mint flavors has made it a summer drink favorite all over the world.

Ingredients

  • White Rum
  • Sugar
  • Lime juice
  • Soda water
  • Mint
